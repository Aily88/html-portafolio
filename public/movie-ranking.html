The Best Movies According to Aily

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pride & Prejudice

This is my favourite period movie. I love the costume design and the ideas they express.

Interstellar

I love this sci-fi adventure movie. The portrayal of communication across space and time is fantastic.

Bridget Jones's Diary

I enjoy this comedy/romantic film a lot. It is one of my favourites in this genre because the principal character fights for his principles and values as a woman.
